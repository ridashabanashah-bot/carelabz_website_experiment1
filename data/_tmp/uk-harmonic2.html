Carelabs is able to detect harmonic distortions early on and take the necessary actions to mitigate their effects in order to ensure the safe operation of the equipment. Harmonics are electric voltages and currents that can degrade the performance of an electrical system. They do not heat well and can degrade power quality. Harmonic voltage analysis is a mathematical technique for forecasting potential resonances and harmonic distortion degrees.

Harmonic voltage analysis is a mathematical approach that uses data to anticipate probable resonances and levels of harmonic distortion. AC variable frequency drives, DC drives, fluorescent light ballasts, and computers are examples of devices that introduce harmonic frequencies to the electrical system. When the harmonic voltage and current levels are high, the likelihood of equipment failure is considerable. Therefore, it is essential to study and evaluate harmonics at the appropriate moment. Current harmonic limitations in 415-volt systems are 5%, with 4% of harmonics being odd and 2% even.
